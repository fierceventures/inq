iNQ is North Queensland's premier "Innovation Centre". We foster collaboration amongst a community of like-minded, passionate people with the aim of becoming the driving force behind the growth and success of North Queensland’s startup ecosystem.

iNQ came from the startup industry FOR startup industry. It is both an institution and a culture of practice, set in Northern Australia’s largest city, close to islands, beaches, airports and business precincts.

Townsville is an affordable regional city in live and work in, with cost of living lower than in capital cities, making your startup dollars go further.

Facilities include permanent desk and offices, hot desks, meeting facilities, high speed broadband, kitchen, storage lockers, balcony entertaining area, break out lounge areas, located in entertainment precinct (movie theatre, cafes, restaurant, gym, serviced apartments)

iNQ is emerging in the Townsville innovation ecosystem as a catalyst site for economic, knowledge and social growth.

With networks reaching around the globe, iNQ’s location in a vibrant mixed use precinct offers potential for growth in tech, creative, education, sustainable development and more.
