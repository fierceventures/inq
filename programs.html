iNQ Programs

iNQ delivers a range of programs to nurture, support and grow the creative and innovative outputs of our community.

Startup Weekend

The hardest part of starting up is starting out. At iNQ Startup Weekend events, you'll be immersed in the ideal environment for startup magic to happen.

Hackathons

Hackathons are not only for programmers or ‘hackers’, they’re an experience which everyone should try. Hackathons inspire people to become change makers as well as being fun, learning and networking experiences.

Pitch Events

Monthly pitch events geared toward startups based in or operating out of Townsville.

Mentor Blaze Events

It’s simple really. We split the 2 hour event into 6x 20-minute time slots, and then mentees book a few mentors they want to meet. The fast feedback on the planet!

Accelerator Programs

INQ is committing partnering (curating) with class leading programs and talent - on local, national and international levels.

Internships

iNQ partners with JCU to encourage their students to experience work in growing companies that are innovative and actually interesting!
